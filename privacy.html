TubeForge — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

1. Who this covers

TubeForge is a personal, self-hosted application operated by a single individual. It has no public sign-up and does not collect data from the general public. The only person whose data it handles is the operator.

2. What data is handled

  • Authentication tokens. When the operator connects a social media account (e.g., TikTok or YouTube) via OAuth, the resulting access tokens are stored locally on the operator's own computer. They are used solely to publish the operator's videos to the operator's accounts.
  • Content and metadata. Videos, captions, and titles created by the application are stored locally on the operator's computer until published.

3. What is NOT collected

TubeForge does not collect personal information from viewers or any third parties, does not use analytics trackers or advertising identifiers, does not sell or share data with anyone, and does not transmit data anywhere except to the platform APIs used for publishing (TikTok, YouTube) and the content services used during production.

4. Data retention and deletion

All stored data lives on the operator's own machine and can be deleted by the operator at any time by removing the application's data folder or revoking the connected accounts' access in the respective platform's security settings (e.g., TikTok → Settings → Security → Manage app permissions).
